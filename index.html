Designing for Impact

Over the past year I collaborated with B Team and Sustrans to create a Power BI dashboard for the Walking and Cycling Index. The project followed a full user-centered design process with research, workshops and testing to ensure policy-makers could easily explore behaviours and infrastructure needs. This experience showed me the importance of UX in data visualisation and helped produce a more intuitive, impactful tool that is now live and in use.

Calculation in Tableau

Calculations in Tableau can be tricky when results aren’t immediately obvious. I make them visible using simple charts and carefully test Level of Detail (LOD) calculations. For complex formulas, I break them into smaller parts to validate each section individually. These techniques boost confidence in results, simplify debugging, and ensure clarity for documentation, collaboration, and future maintenance of all Tableau dashboards effectively.
